(1) Installation:

    - unpack archive
    $ tar -xjvf gmaps.tar.bz2
    
    - copy gmaps source files to your component folder in your project
    $ cp -r ./gmaps ~/www/<your-nette-project>/app/components/
    
    - clear your temp directory
    $ rm -rf ~/www/<your-nette-project>/app/temp/*
